Our Mission

Blending learning, teaching, and volunteering to inspire curiosity and empower students to use STEM as a tool for discovery.

The STEMBound Vision

STEMBound is a student-led organization focused on exploring science, technology, engineering, and mathematics in creative ways. We prioritize hands-on projects, skill-building in coding, and fostering student-led research that makes a tangible impact.

Our Journey

2025

Clothing Drive

Due to the Guadalupe Flood in Texas, STEMBound decided to take action and hold a clothing drive. We collected over 100 t-shirts, pants, and clothing pieces to donate to the people who were affected by the devastating flood.

2026

Toy & Book Drive

For the Christmas season in 2025, STEMBound wanted to give back to the community and help out kids suffering through cancer. We collected over 500 toys and books to be donated at the Scottish Rite Children's Hospital in Frisco through combined efforts from our chapter and officer team. This was possible through our partnerships with Swig, Bahama Buck's, Tropical Smoothie Cafe, Jersey Mike's, and communities across Prosper & Celina!

2026

Olivia Zhang Q&A Session

STEMBound received the opportunity to invite the founder of the largest nonprofit (Cancer Kids First) and Forbes 30 for 30 member, Olivia Zhang! She is an influential figure who attends Harvard University with a large impact. Students asked questions about colleges, career, and academics during this session.
