Our Projects

See how STEMBound gives back to the community and makes an impact through our service initiatives.

Guadalupe River Clothing Drive

In response to flooding near the Guadalupe River, STEMBound organized a clothing drive and collected over 200 clothing pieces to give back to those affected by the disaster.

Dallas Area Toy & Book Drive

We collected over 500 toys across 5 businesses and 5–7 communities, donating them to the Scottish Rite for Children in Frisco to bring joy to children in need.
